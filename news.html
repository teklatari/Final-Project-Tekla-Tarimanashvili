Our Inspirations

Irakli Charkviani

Poet of rebellion, voice of a generation.

Known for his poetic realism and experimental sound, Irakli shaped our band's identity. His influence runs through our cover of “Menatreba” and inspires our lyrics and live energy.

Pink Floyd

Dream architects. Space and soul blended.

Their cosmic sound and immersive concepts inspired the atmospheric layers of “Eight Dimension.” “Breathe In the Air” helped shape how we design emotions sonically.

Sade

Smooth, magnetic, eternal.

Her soft-jazz aura influenced our slower ballads like “Moonless.” Covering “Smooth Operator” was a chance to echo her elegance in our tone and rhythm.

The Beatles

Every melody owes them something.

Their harmonies and hooks built our songwriting roots. “Let It Be” wasn’t just a concert song—it was the emotional end of our school concert chapter.
