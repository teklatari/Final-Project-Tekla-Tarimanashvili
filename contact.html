Who We Are

We’re 810 — the band born out of passion, noise, and raw school energy.
We don’t just play music — we live it. This site is our digital stage. Because we’ve got something to say — and we’re saying it loud.

We’re here. We’re loud. We’re original.

Why We Made This Site

Too many school bands stay in the shadows. No website, no platform, no voice.
We're flipping the script. This is our space — made by teens, for teens.
